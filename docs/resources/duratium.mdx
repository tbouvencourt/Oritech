Duratium is an alloy composed of [platinum](../resources/platinum) and netherite, which can only be produced in a [foundry](../processing/foundry).

It is known for its high durability and heat resistance.

This powerful alloy is utilized in the construction of the [atomic forge](../processing/atomic_forge), [bedrock extractor](../interaction/bedrock_extractor),
and the ultra (tier 6) machine core.
